L-Citrulline 9,000mg
Pump ▾
L-Citrulline converts to L-Arginine in the kidneys, boosting nitric oxide production which causes vasodilation — more blood flow, bigger pumps, better nutrient delivery. At 9g, Gorilla Mode nearly doubles the clinical dose, making it one of the highest-dosed citrulline products on the market.
Effectiveness★★★★★

Caffeine Anhydrous 350mg
Stimulant ▾
The dehydrated, fast-absorbing form of caffeine — roughly equivalent to 3.5 cups of coffee. Works by blocking adenosine receptors to keep you alert and focused. At 350mg, not for beginners or low-caffeine tolerant individuals.
Effectiveness★★★★★
Learn More About Caffeine Anhydrous →
Alpha GPC 300mg
Focus ▾
Crosses the blood-brain barrier and increases acetylcholine — the neurotransmitter for muscle contractions and cognition. Result: sharper mind-muscle connection, better focus, and measurable improvements in power output. 300mg is the clinical dose.
Effectiveness★★★★☆
Learn More About Alpha GPC →
Beta-Alanine 3,200mg
Endurance ▾
Combines with histidine to form carnosine, which buffers lactic acid in muscles and delays the burn. The tingling sensation (paresthesia) is harmless and means it's working. 3.2g is the exact clinical dose.
Effectiveness★★★★☆
Learn More About Beta-Alanine →
Creatine Monohydrate 5,000mg
Performance ▾
The most researched supplement in sports science — 500+ peer-reviewed studies. Replenishes ATP faster for more power output and less fatigue. 5g daily is the gold standard dose. Including it means you don't need a separate creatine supplement.
Effectiveness★★★★★
Learn More About Creatine Monohydrate →
L-Tyrosine 2,500mg
Focus ▾
L-Tyrosine is a building block for dopamine, adrenaline, and noradrenaline. During intense exercise these deplete rapidly. Supplementing replenishes the supply, maintaining mental clarity and motivation deeper into your session.
Effectiveness★★★★☆
Learn More About L-Tyrosine →
Betaine Anhydrous 2,000mg
Performance ▾
Betaine is a methyl donor involved in numerous metabolic pathways. As an osmolyte it draws water into cells improving hydration. Multiple studies show 2.5g/day over several weeks significantly improves muscular endurance, power output, and body composition.
Effectiveness★★★★☆
Learn More About Betaine Anhydrous →
HydroPrime® Glycerol Powder 2,000mg
Pump ▾
HydroPrime® is the most bioavailable form of glycerol at 65% concentration. Glycerol draws water into cells (osmolyte), increasing intracellular hydration and muscle cell volume — creating a dense, full-looking pump. Especially effective for high-intensity or hot-weather training.
Effectiveness★★★★☆
Learn More About HydroPrime® Glycerol Powder →
Malic Acid 1,500mg
Performance ▾
Malic acid is an intermediate in the Krebs cycle — the process your cells use to generate ATP (energy). Most commonly bonded to citrulline as Citrulline Malate, where it enhances the pump and endurance benefits. On its own it may reduce exercise-induced fatigue and support recovery.
Effectiveness★★★☆☆
Learn More About Malic Acid →
Pink Himalayan Sea Salt 500mg
Electrolytes ▾
Pink Himalayan salt provides sodium along with trace amounts of potassium, magnesium, and calcium. Electrolytes are critical for muscle contractions, nerve signaling, and fluid balance — especially during high-sweat sessions. A small but meaningful addition.
Effectiveness★★★☆☆
Learn More About Pink Himalayan Sea Salt →
Alpha-GPC 50% 400mg
Focus ▾
Alpha GPC converts to acetylcholine in the brain — the primary neurotransmitter for muscle contractions and cognition. In the gym, this means better mind-muscle connection, sharper focus, and improved reaction time. Studies show 600mg can significantly increase peak bench press power output.
Effectiveness★★★★★
Learn More About Alpha-GPC 50% →
Huperzine A 100mcg
Focus ▾
Huperzine A prevents the breakdown of acetylcholine by inhibiting acetylcholinesterase. This prolongs and amplifies the effects of Alpha GPC and other choline sources, resulting in enhanced memory, focus, and mind-muscle connection. Requires cycling — don't use daily for extended periods.
Effectiveness★★★★☆
